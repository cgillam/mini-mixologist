Lets craft a cocktail
Welcome to Mini Mixologist. This app will turn the average Joe in to a master mixologist in just a few clicks. Below you will be able select a combination of whatever alcohols you wish to combine. After you make your we will generate a list of all possible cocktails that are available to you with a simple step by step guide that will teach you to turn your dusty old bottles into carefully crafted libations.
You may select up to four ingredients in the drop down menus below. When you are satisfied with your choices select make my drink and we will show you a list of options for you to chose from.
